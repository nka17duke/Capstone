Welcome to the Durham Lead Contamination Prevention Committee

The City of Durham is addressing lead contamination in several local parks, where elevated levels were detected due to historical waste disposal. The city is working with environmental agencies to test and remediate affected areas, prioritizing public health and safety. Residents, especially families with young children, are encouraged to stay informed and take advantage of free lead testing resources.
