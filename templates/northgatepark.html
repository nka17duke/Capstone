Northgate Park

Throughout Northgate Park most areas had lead below the safe limit, but one spot under the playground mulch had a high level (1,430 mg/kg), which is likely an isolated case. A few other spots near roads had slightly elevated lead (above 280 mg/kg but below 400 mg/kg), possibly from old car exhaust. There were also sightings of arsenic, manganese, and cobalt, but they match natural levels in North Carolina soil, not enough to be considered pollution.
