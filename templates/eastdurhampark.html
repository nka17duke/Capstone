East Durham Park

Around East Durham park there were several areas that had lead over 400 mg/kg, these areas were split between a field in the southeast of the park near homes, and a spot in the western area of the park by a shelter. Deeper samples revealed worse lead (up to 3,180 mg/kg), with ash fragments suggesting a history of dumping. In higher lead areas there were also metals like cadmium and copper.
