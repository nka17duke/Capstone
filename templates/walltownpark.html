Walltown Park

Within Walltown park, eight spots had lead above 400 mg/kg, teetering the edge of the safe limit. Most of these spots were found around a creek in the park, with one being found by a basketball court (459 mg/kg). Deeper soil samples showed even higher lead (up to 3,480 mg/kg), traces of glass or porcelain bits hinted at old incinerator ash as the culprit. There were also unusually high arsenic levels in one spot (16.8 mg/kg).
