About This Study

This project is done by students from North Carolina Central Universtiy's School of Business in collaboration with the Durham Committee on the Affairs of Black People Inc. to create a platform for members of the Durham community to have a place to voice their opionion, get informed, and feel a sense of togetherness in a trying situation.
