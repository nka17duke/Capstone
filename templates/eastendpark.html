East End Park

Inside East End park there were three spots that exceeded the safe limit, two of them being found in a restricted area. The third (772 mg/kg) was found near an old building, but that may have been caused by peeling paint. Deeper samples revealed nothing out of the ordinary and the playground was deemed safe. There were slightly high Arsenic and cobalt levels but not outside of natural limits.
